ABOUT US

We perform intelligent data analysis

Damavis is an innovative studio specialized in the management of the most important resource of 21st century companies: their data. Our main goal is to get the highest performance of the data so that our clients can make better strategic business decisions, going hand by hand with them on the way to becoming a Data Driven company. The high versatility of our profiles makes it possible for us to provide our services in any area related to data. We manage from the creation of optimized Data Lakes to the deployment of artificial intelligence algorithms into live production environments whose reponses can happen in real time.

At Damavis we believe that the journey towards Digital Transformation must be progressive and complete, adapted to the needs of each of our clients. With our experience in the data world, we are able to apply scalable, flexible and adapted solutions to all types of markets.

ABOUT US

Damavis history

We know your problem, and we know that there is a solution. And not just a solution, but an ability to add value to what you already have and ensure a resounding success for your business. And that power resides in the data.

Our CEO David Martin has dedicated his life to the world of the big data and artificial intelligence, and in 2018 decided to focus on your vision and create something authentic, quality and above all effective.

To achieve this, brought together the best, the team that had been working for years and together they created the company Aneior Studio SL, where they recorded their study of Big Data and Machine Learning with the name of Damavis.

Within days, thanks to the good reputation of quality that the team had achieved began to work for their first customers.

The effectiveness, efficiency and overall quality with which Damavis solve the problems of its customers, led to success stories that served as an example and inspiration for new customers.

Today, we have the trust of customers as Barceló, W2M, Camper, Jumbo Tours, FastPay, Globalia, Avoris, among others. Our goal remains focused on being the benchmark for quality and talent of the Big Data, being a safe bet for the future and success, highlighting our commitment to the customer, the team and society.

What a customer for a problem or hindrance to progress, Damavis perceives it as a possibility, and transforms, packed in a case of success.
